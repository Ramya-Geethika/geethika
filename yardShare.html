YardShare YEG

The initial page is the home page, that explains the importance of YardSharing. Next will be the connect yards page, I have added a post a listing button, where the Landholders and Growers can fill their details. Have also added a drop down option to categorize either Landholder or Grower. In gardening guide page have added links to the resources which could be the reference for Landholders and Growers.

Have used React Leaflet map with two icons a plant representing Growers and SaveEarth icon representing Landholders.Through the ContactUs page, if someone would like to enquire about YardSharing they can submit the form by filling their details.

To build this application I have used React,SASS in front end and Express, postgreSQL in back end
